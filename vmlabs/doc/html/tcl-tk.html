Tcl/Tk 8.1

The PUFFIN Debugger and certain other tools in the NUON SDK require that version 8.1 of the Tcl/TK scripting language & graphics toolkit be installed on your system. 

Tcl/Tk may needed to be installed separately.  If so, please locate and run:

\VMLABS\TCL811.EXE
