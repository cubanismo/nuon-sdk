Development System RAM Configuration Info

VM Labs SDK Internal Release 0.86.2
June 14, 2001

Here are instructions for how to jumper a development system down to 8MB.

Some of you may not already know that NUON development platforms have 16 MB of DRAM on the system bus, but most production NUON systems will have only 8 MB there. Note that certain production systems do have 16 MB of DRAM, such as the Samsung N2000 Extiva-1.

On a rev 5.3 system (metal case reference player, aka "clearcase")

Jumper U56 (3-pin header at the bottom right of the board)

jumper across pins 1-2
 8MB
jumper across pins 2-3
 16MB

Pin 1 is nearest the corner of the board 2.

On a rev 5.1 system (PC housing developer system, aka "whitebox")

Jumper U56 (3-pin header near NUON)

jumper across pins 1-2
 8MB
jumper across pins 2-3
 16MB

Pin 1 is nearest the joystick ports on the top/back of the board
